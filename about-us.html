Our Story

2015 - The Beginning

Founded in Kyiv with a vision to create premium caps that reflect Ukrainian style and quality. Started with just 3 local artisans.

2018 - Workshop Expansion

Opened our main manufacturing facility in Kyiv, employing 25+ local craftsmen and introducing modern production techniques.

2020 - National Recognition

Became Ukraine's leading cap manufacturer, serving customers across the country and gaining international attention.

2024 - Global Reach

Proudly representing Ukrainian craftsmanship worldwide while maintaining our commitment to local production and quality.
